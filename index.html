vrse has verve.

By definition, verve means enthusiasm or vigor, as in literary or artistic work; spirit. This is the 6th anniversary of the calendar for hope and as every year past, it has been put together with great vigor and enthusiasm for preserving the memory of Christopher and raising awareness about the importance of organ & tissue donation.

vrse has history.

verve 2011 is the 6th anniversary of the calendar for hope. The idea was born in 2005 when Jess created a small homemade calendar for family and friends. Entitled Tonal 2006, the calendar featured Jess' art and was made in memory of her little brother, Chris. Chris was nine years old when he passed away – he was waiting for a liver transplant.

A close friend who received the calendar wanted to purchase calendars to give to her friends—alas, six additional copies of Tonal 2006 were produced and sold for $6 each. The $36 was donated to U of A's HOPE program and the calendar for hope was born. (A heartfelt thank you to Amy, who unknowingly sparked something so wonderful).

Since that first year, vrse design inc. has been committed to making the calendar for hope an annual fundraiser in memory of Christopher. Every year, we produce and sell an art-filled calendar with 100% of the proceeds donated to the University of Alberta's Human Organ Procurement and Exchange (HOPE) Program to help raise awareness about the importance of becoming an organ & tissue donor.

vrse has purpose.

This is the most rewarding design project we do and we thank you—our buyers, sponsors, guest artists, clients, friends and families for your generosity and support. This calendar is successful because people like you make it so.

To date, vrse has sold 830 calendars and raised and donated over $9,000 to the University of Alberta Hospital’s HOPE Foundation. We hope to make it to 1180 calendars and $12,500 with the sales of verve 2011.

Cheers! Jess & Joanne | vrse design inc.
